Meet the makers

Roël Couwenberg

I'm Roël Couwenberg, a flexible all-round developer and designer from Rosmalen, the Netherlands.

Ever since I was little I've had a passion for all konds of technology, the idea that you could make practically anything using it caught my eyes. This is the reason why in 2013 I began programming in Java, JavaScript and Python.

This passion eventually led to me being able to start my own web and graphic design agency Avametix and since then I've been proud to say this is my job.
